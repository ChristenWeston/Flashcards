Functions:

Functions: A function is something that performs an action. Just like a method, a function can take an argument.

Methods:

Methods: A method is a kind of function that is called on something.

Arguments:

Arguments: Both functions and methods can have arguments. Arguments are passed into the parentheses (). It's not required for a method to have arguments. Some take no arguments, some take optional arguments, and some take one or even many arguments.
